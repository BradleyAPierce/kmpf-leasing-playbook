Leasing Foundations

Understanding the fundamentals of leasing is essential for every sales professional. This module covers the key concepts, terminology, and value propositions that make leasing an attractive option for customers.

Why Leasing Matters

Customer Benefits
  • Preserve capital and maintain cash flow
  • Predictable monthly payments
  • Tax advantages and potential write-offs
  • Upgrade technology regularly
  • Avoid equipment obsolescence
Sales Benefits
  • Overcome price objections
  • Larger deal sizes with manageable payments
  • Competitive advantage in negotiations
  • Recurring revenue opportunities
  • Strengthen customer relationships

Lifecycle of a Lease

The Lifecycle of a Lease Wheel gives you a quick, clear view of how a lease moves from the very first conversation to renewal. Each stage shows what needs to happen, who's involved, and how your actions keep the process moving.

Understanding this lifecycle helps you ask better questions, set the right expectations, and guide your customer with confidence. When you know how the lease process works from start to finish, you can avoid delays, improve the customer experience, and uncover more upgrade and renewal opportunities.
