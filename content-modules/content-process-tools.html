Essential Resources, Actions, and Tools

This section outlines key activities to complete at the start of each quarter, such as updating rates and promotions. Following these steps keeps your proposals accurate and aligned with the latest programs. Click the provided links below to gain quick access.

Actions at start of each quarter

  1. Download new KMPF rate cards from MyKM at: MyKM → Marketing → Direct Channel Marketing → Sales Tools
    • Direct Production Print
    • Direct Standard Rates
    • GPO (Group Purchasing Organization) Rates
  2. Bookmark calendar reminder to download new rates at the start of each quarter
  3. Download current promotions from MyKM at: MyKM → Marketing → Direct Channel Marketing → Customer Promotions
    • Solutions
    • Office Print
    • Production Print and Industrial Print

Actions for weekly and monthly monitoring

  1. Check updates via weekly Monday morning DCM (Direct Channel Marketing ) Sales News & Updates in your email
  2. Submit promotion exceptions through DCM SharePoint file
    3. Note: RLMs cannot approve exceptions
