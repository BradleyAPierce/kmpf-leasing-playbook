Conversation Guide to Winning with Leasing Solutions

This document is designed to give you confidence in every leasing conversation. Focus on understanding customer priorities first, then matching KMPF benefits to their specific needs. Remember that successful leasing conversations are about solving business problems, not just offering financing options.

Why Leasing Matters

Customer Benefits
  • Preserve capital and maintain cash flow
  • Predictable monthly payments
  • Tax advantages and potential write-offs
  • Upgrade technology regularly
  • Avoid equipment obsolescence
Sales Benefits
  • Overcome price objections
  • Larger deal sizes with manageable payments
  • Competitive advantage in negotiations
  • Recurring revenue opportunities
  • Strengthen customer relationships
